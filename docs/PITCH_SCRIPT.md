# Classroom 2.0 — 2-Minute Pitch Script

Practice this exact tap path on one device. The Demo Mode chip + role switcher means everything below works without Firebase, internet, or a second phone.

---

## 0:00 – 0:15 · Problem

> Classrooms still rely on manual attendance and passive lectures.
> Professors often do not know who is actually present or who understood the lesson until it's too late.

## 0:15 – 0:30 · Solution

> We built **Classroom 2.0**, a native Android app that turns any class into a live learning experience.
> QR attendance, live quizzes, AI explanations, and real-time teaching insights — all on one screen.

## 0:30 – 1:15 · Live Demo

> *(On the professor dashboard)* Here's the professor dashboard. In one tap, the professor starts an attendance session and gets a session-specific QR.
>
> *(Tap Start attendance — countdown + QR appear)*
>
> The student scans the QR and is checked in instantly. The professor sees attendance update live.
>
> *(Switch to Student view → Scan attendance QR → Demo Scan → animated success screen)*
>
> Now the professor launches a live quiz.
>
> *(Switch back to Pro view → Start live quiz → Use demo question → Start quiz)*
>
> The student answers from their phone, and the professor immediately sees the class result distribution.
>
> *(Switch to Student view → tap correct answer → Submit. Switch back to Pro view → bars animate in)*

## 1:15 – 1:40 · Extras

> The app goes beyond the required features.
>
> *(Tap View insight)* Professors get a **Teaching Insight Dashboard** that recommends what to explain next.
>
> *(Switch to Student view → AI explainer → polymorphism)* Students can use the **AI Concept Explainer** to understand difficult topics — four styles, all offline.
>
> *(Tap Leaderboard)* Students also earn points and badges. The Leaderboard creates motivation and engagement.

## 1:40 – 1:55 · Technical Quality

> Classroom 2.0 is built with **Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, Material 3, MVVM, and a Firebase / in-memory backend abstraction**, so it works both as a real product and as a reliable hackathon demo.

## 1:55 – 2:00 · Closing

> Classroom 2.0 replaces passive classrooms with interactive, measurable, AI-assisted learning.

---

## Backup Plan

- If the camera is unreliable, the **Demo Scan** button always works.
- If Firebase is misconfigured, the **Demo Mode** chip is your signal — every screen still works on the in-memory backend.
- If the role switcher feels slow, just rehearse the tap path twice before going live.

Keep the pitch under 2 minutes. The judges should think: *this feels like a real product, not a school assignment.*
